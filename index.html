I’m a Software Engineer with a front-end focus, a background in design, and an eagerness to learn.

My graphic design skills have definitely influenced my approach to problem-solving and execution of web development projects, specifically in terms of being mindful of UX/UI while achieving the desired function of the finished product. In 2018, I decided to expand my graphic design career by learning web design, and so enrolled in Case Western Reserve University’s 24-week Full-Stack Coding Bootcamp. I was surprised how much I enjoyed the challenges and gratification of web development, so my intention of "upskilling" became instead a full-on career change when I joined the Columbus, OH branch of WillowTree Apps (formerly Dynamit.)

Since then, I've continued my rapid growth of coding knowledge and implementation through a variety of client projects, participating in coding meetup groups, attending virtual conferences, and creating a lengthy set of goals for myself. I'm also passionate about increasing the number of under-represented groups in software engineering and technology, particularly women and non-binary engineers, and participate in college recruiting/networking events to answer future engineers' questions about working as a woman in tech.

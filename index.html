I’m a Front-End Web Developer with a strong background in graphic design.

My graphic design skills have definitely influenced my approach to problem-solving and execution of web development projects, specifically in terms of being mindful of UX/UI while achieving the overall look and function of the finished product.

I earned my certificate of Full-Stack Web Development through Case Western Reserve University’s 24-week Coding Bootcamp. During my time there, I developed my JavaScript, jQuery, React, HTML, and CSS skills by creating many apps, but found I learned the most from the in-depth group projects. I am continuously learning and implementing new CSS frameworks and JS libraries. My graphic design skills, combined with bootcamp knowledge, have set me up to thrive in a career in user-focused, Front-End Web Development.
